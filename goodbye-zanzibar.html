Good-Bye Zanzibar

Sat 11 November 2023

Just wrapped up the last day at #DjangoConAfrica and what a whirlwind it has been! The schedule was tight, but boy, we did pack in a ton of knowledge and inspiration. 🙌

Despite the rainy weather dampening our plans to tour Stone Town, I'm grateful for the incredible discussions and connections made right at the conference center. Rain or shine, the Django community shines bright! ✨

But hey, I managed to seize a moment and squeeze in a quick trip to Forodani beach before saying my goodbyes. 🏖️ Nothing like the sound of crashing waves and the feeling of warm sand between your toes to rejuvenate the spirit.

As I bid farewell to this amazing event, I am filled with gratitude for the enlightening talks, the laughs shared, and the immense talent that surrounded us. Thank you! 💙
